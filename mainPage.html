Choose your Starter Pokémon!

Your first Pokémon is one of the most important decisions a new Trainer can make. Your starter will influence the rest of your journey from this moment onwards. So it's paramount you make the right decision.

This website is here to help you make the most informed choice possible, so make sure to read all the information we have availalble.

Charmander



Charmander, the Lizard Pokémon has always been a fan favorite. The perfect mix of cute and cool appeals to trainers of all personalities however just because it's popular doens't mean it's the best. Charmander's typing and overall abilities actually make it less than ideal for pokemon leauge battles. Fire has many weaknesses and only a few strengths over the other powerful Pokémon and it's stats even when evolved don't make up for those weaknesses.

Pros

  • Good design
  • Type Advantage over Grass, Bug, Steel, Ice
  • Can fly you around when evolved

Cons

  • Weak to Water, Electric, Rock, Ground
  • Medicore stats all around
  • Very common choice
Check out Charmander's Page for more Info!

Squirtle




Squirtle, the Tiny Turtle Pokémon is a classic. A trusty water type with good defense thanks to it's trusty shell. Water doesn't have too many weaknesses aside from the obvious electric which make it an ever better defensive choice. But offensively it doesn't offer much coverage either.

Pros

  • Defense
  • Type Advantage over Fire, Rock, Ground
  • Can learn Surf

Cons

  • Weak to Electric and Grass Type Moves
  • Medicore in terms of offense
Check out Squirtle's Page for more Info!

Bulbasaur




Bulbasaur, the seed Pokémon is a true underrated gem. It's typing and moveset make it invaluable on a balance team needed to take on the Pokémon leauge and it has the flexibility to fill a fw niches.

Pros

  • Good Defense
  • Type Advantage over Water, Psychic, Rock, Ground
  • Has access to status moves
  • Has access to HP recovery moves
  • A unique choice

Cons

  • Weak to Fire, Ice, Flying, Psychic
  • Status moves often miss
  • Can't fly or surf
Check out Bulbasaur's Page for more Info!
